Case Study

Complete brand identity for a specialty coffee roaster expanding from local to national.

The Challenge

Bloom needed a brand that captured their artisanal roots while appealing to a broader market. Their existing identity felt dated and inconsistent across channels.

Our Approach

  • Competitor and market analysis
  • Customer journey mapping
  • Iterative design sprints

Brand Identity

Logo, color system, typography, imagery guidelines

Packaging

Retail bags, wholesale, merchandise

Digital

Website, mobile app, social templates

Environmental

Store design, signage, menu boards
